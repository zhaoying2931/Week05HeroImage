The Rusted Land

— A Nostalgic Journey into Central Pennsylvania —

An interactive story about how Pennsylvania redefines "Rust Belt".

Historically, the term "Rust Belt" has been used to characterized a portion of the Northeastern and Midwestern U.S. plagued by a decling poplution, a weakedn economy, adn urban decay due to a faltering industrial sector. Pennsylvania traditionally has been lumped into this category, particularly by the media. But times have changed, and so have we. Dennis Davin
